# Website Theme: The Yosemite Trail

The website represents my personal and professional journey as a hike through Yosemite. Visitors should feel like they are exploring a trail rather than scrolling through a traditional portfolio.

The design should be calm, modern, and trustworthy, using neutral earth tones, generous whitespace, smooth animations, and subtle nature-inspired elements. Avoid anything that feels like a game, travel blog, or cartoon.

The experience follows a winding trail:

* **Trailhead** → Hero and introduction
* **Forest** → About me and motivation
* **Trail Markers** → Experience and education
* **Campsites** → Projects (each project is a stop along the trail)
* **Gear** → Skills and technologies I've collected
* **Scenic Overlooks** → Major achievements and milestones
* **Summit** → Current goals, resume, and contact information

A subtle trail should visually connect each section as the user scrolls. Microinteractions should feel smooth and intentional, creating the sense of progressing along a journey.

The overall goal is to communicate curiosity, continuous growth, craftsmanship, and professionalism while making visitors feel like they are discovering my story.
